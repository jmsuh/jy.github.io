What We Do

Research Interests

🧬
AI & Healthcare Big Data
Developing AI technologies utilizing large-scale medical big data from national health insurance databases.
📊
Disease Prediction Models
Building machine learning models to predict the onset of chronic diseases including diabetes, stroke, and cancer.
🔬
Hidden Markov Random Field
Statistical modeling using HMRF and Bayesian MCMC algorithms for spatial and temporal health data.
💡
Multiple Testing
Semiparametric methods for large-scale simultaneous hypothesis testing under dependency structures.
🏥
Epidemiology & Biostatistics
Risk factors and outcomes for cardiovascular disease, metabolic syndrome, and population health.
🤖
Machine Learning Methods
Survival models, propensity score methods, and deep learning applications for clinical data.
